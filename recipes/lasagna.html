Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough (or homemade if you like)
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese (or cottage cheese for lighter version)
  • 1 egg (optional, helps bind ricotta mixture)
  • 3 cups fresh spinach (or 1 cup frozen spinach, thawed and drained)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2–3 cups marinara or tomato pasta sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • A pinch of nutmeg (optional, adds warmth to spinach filling)

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Prepare spinach mixture
    • Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté garlic (optional).
    • Add spinach and cook until wilted (if using fresh).
    • Let it cool slightly, then mix with ricotta, egg, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  3. Cook noodles
    • If using regular lasagna noodles, boil them until just al dente, then drain.
    • If using no-boil noodles, skip this step.
  4. Layer the lasagna
    • Spread a thin layer of sauce at the bottom of a baking dish.
    • Add noodles → spinach ricotta mixture → sauce → mozzarella.
    • Repeat until you use everything (usually 3 layers).
    • Top layer: noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  5. Bake
    • Cover with foil (to prevent cheese burning).
    • Bake at 190°C (375°F) for 25 minutes.
    • Remove foil and bake another 10–15 minutes until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  6. Rest & serve
    • Let it sit for 10 minutes before slicing (helps hold shape).
