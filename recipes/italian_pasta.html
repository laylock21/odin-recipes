Lasagna

Italian pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g (about 2 handfuls) spaghetti
  • 3–4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional, for spice)
  • Salt (for pasta water)
  • Fresh parsley (chopped, optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, not traditional but tasty)

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Cook pasta
    • Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
    • Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  3. Make sauce
    • In a pan, heat olive oil on medium-low.
    • If using no-boil noodles, skip this step.
  4. Layer the lasagna
    • Spread a thin layer of sauce at the bottom of a baking dish.
    • Add garlic slices and cook until golden (don’t burn!).
    • Add red pepper flakes (if using).
    • Top layer: noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  5. Combine
    • Add drained pasta into the pan with garlic oil.
    • Toss well, adding a splash of reserved pasta water to coat.
  6. Finish & serve
    • Sprinkle parsley on top.
    • Add Parmesan if you like.
    • Serve hot!
