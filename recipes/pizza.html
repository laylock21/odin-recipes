Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough (or homemade if you like)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce (store-bought or tomato sauce with Italian seasoning)
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 12–15 slices pepperoni
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (optional, for brushing crust)
  • A pinch of dried oregano or basil (optional)

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Prepare dough: Roll out the pizza dough into a round shape on a lightly floured surface. Place on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Add sauce: Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add cheese: Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Add pepperoni: Arrange pepperoni slices evenly across the pizza.
  6. Season & brush (optional): Lightly brush crust edges with olive oil and sprinkle oregano/basil.
  7. Bake: Place in the oven for 12–15 minutes, or until cheese is melted and crust is golden brown.
  8. Slice & serve: Let it cool for 2 minutes, slice, and enjoy!
